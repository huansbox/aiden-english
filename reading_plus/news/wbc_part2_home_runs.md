# Home Runs Back and Forth

**Who will take the lead next?**

1 Korea's pitcher, Ryu, kept the game close after Chang's home run. He did not let Chinese Taipei score again for a long time.

2 Then, in the sixth inning, Cheng hit a solo home run. The ball sailed over the wall. Chinese Taipei was ahead again, 2-1! The fans waved their flags and cheered.

3 But Korea's young star, Kim, was not done. He stepped up and crushed a two-run home run. Korea took the lead, 3-2. Some fans covered their eyes.

4 In the eighth inning, Fairchild came to bat for Chinese Taipei. He swung and sent the ball deep into the seats. Two runs scored! Chinese Taipei was ahead, 4-3. The crowd went wild.

5 Then Kim came up again. He hit a double off the wall. A runner scored. The game was tied, 4-4!

6 After nine innings, the score was still tied. The game went to extra innings. Nobody wanted to look away.
