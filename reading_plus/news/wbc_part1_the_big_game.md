# The Big Game

**Can a team that has never won change everything in one day?**

1 It was a sunny Sunday in Tokyo. More than 40,000 fans filled the Tokyo Dome. Most of them were from Taiwan. They came to cheer for their team, Chinese Taipei.

2 Chinese Taipei was playing against Korea in the World Baseball Classic. Countries from all over the world play in this big tournament. But Chinese Taipei never beat Korea before. Not even once!

3 Cheerleaders danced on top of the dugouts. A band played music. The whole stadium felt like a party. But the fans were nervous, too. Chinese Taipei had lost their first games. If they lost again, they would go home.

4 Then the game began. In the second inning, Yu Chang stepped up to bat. He swung hard and sent the ball flying over the wall. Home run! The crowd jumped to their feet and screamed. Chinese Taipei was ahead, 1-0.

5 "We can do this!" the fans shouted. The team captain, Chen, clapped his hands in the dugout. This game was just getting started.
